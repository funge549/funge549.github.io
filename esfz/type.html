"FANCY FREE" Redesigned Book Layout

InDesign + Photoshop | 2019

Prompt: Book as curation, to select a book / catalogue from The Strand and remake such object as a new book
FANCY FREE is a 2013 shoe catalogue for Barneys New York. The redesigned version has the same shoe images and captions. Yet, in this book the elements are
playful with each other, each page is different from the other. The price of each shoe is the focus of each page, creating a focus point in the pricepoint of the shoes.
I switched out the store's "exclusively ours" logo for the phrase "not exclusivelyx ours" to make the viewer question the ideas of luxury, exclusivity and the future department stores.
Typeset in: Roc Grotesk *showcasing digital version





"Colors of Times Square" Book Design & Layout

InDesign | 2019

Prompt: Book as Object, to create a book based on a selection of photographs taken by another classmate. Jiapei took photographs of typography in Times Square. I decided to select images that fell into five different colors and created a dynamic layout that allowed different sizes of images and blocks of color to be placed together. That paired both the images with colored blocks taken from the same photograph. The book's cover is a visual mapping of each photograph that was taken and which color is present there. Typeset in American Typewriter *showcasing digital version





"Honesty & Relatability" Motion Graphics & Book Layout

"New York Times OpEd" Design & Layout

InDesign + Photoshop | 2018

Promt: To pick an op-ed article from the New York Times (published on September 7th, 2019) and create an op-ed illustration that represents the article. The goal was to create an illustration with typography as it's main element. I picked this article where it depicted Kavanaugh's past and weird obsessions with conspiracy theories that involved the President Clinton. Using printed images and articles that were related to the conspiracies and pinned them out on a wall in a way that the word 'cryptic' would be connect by one long piece thread.
