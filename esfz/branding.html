"pechá" Tea Packaging

Illustrator | 2019

Prompt: To create a tea brand that will consist of five SKUs, two loose tea, two tea bags and one novelty item.
"pechá" stands for the combination of peach, the main flavours of this brand, and the romanization of the Chinese word for tea: 茶 'cha'.
But "pechá" isn't a regularly tea brand, all it's flavours are inspired by Brunch styled drinks and aim to be an easier way to enjoy such cocktails.
The different SKUs are easy to spot as they have very different packaging. The loose tea in a tall and skinny box, the silk tea bags in a standard box and the sample set in a drawer style box.




"Costa Dulce" Chocolate Packaging

Illustrator | 2019

Prompt: To create a high-end chocolate brand that consists of four different SKUs.
Costa Rica is known for its finest quality and different flavour profiles and there is also a booming culture of handcrafted chocolates.
Costa Dulce (Sweet Coast) is a pairing of these two products. This luxurious chocolates are made in small batches and to be sold in specialty stores like Dean and Deluca.
The packaging is color coded with the water coloured illustrations that shine in the face of the box.




"fitted" Objects for Inclusion

InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Sketch + Invision | 2018

Prompt: To pick an object and create a new brand that is inclusive to your audience no matter how they identify.
As a 1.60m (5"2') female I have a hard time finding pants / jeans that fit my body type, thus I decided to create a brand
that will focus on how giving smaller size females clothing pieces that will actually fit. Fitted is a timeless, considerate and approachable brand.
The brand applications showcase the whole user experience someone can have with the brand. From the website to the customised packaging to the clothing labels.





"Sundaes and Cones" Rebranding

InDesign, Illustrator + Photoshop | 2018

Prompt: Choose a restaurant whose branding needs improving. Rebuild their brand from the ground up.
Sundaes and Cones is a ice cream parlor in the East Village that offers the classic American flavours as well as novelty Asian influenced ones.
After creating a new brand strategy, the next step was to design the brand assets that will help reinvent and make the brand more playful.
The brand applications go from having the 'sc' pattern on the waffle cone to rethinking the store's floor plan and user flow.
