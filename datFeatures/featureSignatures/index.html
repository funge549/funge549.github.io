dat.signatures

let them know it's you

what?

_ we all want to be heard and we all want others to know it is our voice.
_ dat.signatures is all about letting your fellow network friends that you are the owner of the post. it is beneficial if others are pulling your content into their own sites, this way everyone will know it is your voice.
_ we offer an easy way of uploading your signature, no need to save the file in a specific folder or maintain a file path, your site will keep.
_check these dat.signatures and how to make them!

why?

_ we all wish to share our ideas and creations in the network, but what if your site is run by a collective or you want to be more expressive in your posts.
_ you can express yourself and see your own signature evolve through time. consider this feature as an extension of your creativity and personality dat.signature is for you.



how? aka documentation

_ you are already part of the social network in dat, so with these easy steps you will be able to use this feature within moments.
_ you don't need to contact your programmer "friend", you are able to use this feature and implement it yourself.
_ you will need to have access to your programming files and be able to edit them following this simple tutorial here!
