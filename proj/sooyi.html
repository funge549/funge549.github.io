Exploration of Branding and Self-Entrepreneur

Collaboration: with Stephanie Yoon

This project was created honestly out of boredom between two recent college graduates. We decided to turn our hobby into a micro company on Instagram where we explore the materiality of beads and explore different aesthetics on our feed.



Sooyi is all about expressing yourself and feeling good, I have created several edits and gifs to be used in the Instagram Stories as well in the feed. This has given me the chance to create a new brand system and be more creative with both the product and the posts.
