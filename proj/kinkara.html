Kinkára


Exploration of web interactions for a luxury retreat hospitality project // UI / UX Design

Client:  Kinkára   Collaboration with  <elastica>

This luxury eco resort is one of a kind and it's brand system reflects it. Full of vibrancy, bold gradients and prominent patterns reflective of their location (Borucá Territory in Costa Rica). We reflected all these imagery and personality through an immersive and unique website proposal. Having the website be it's own world, was an extension of the Kinkára experience and give the potential guest a taste of what is waiting for them

Although these proposed sites weren't develop, they did entice and excite the client to hope to create a website like this in the future.
