The Coffee Source


Rebranding + Site refresh // Interaction Design

Client:  The Coffee Source  Collaboration with  <elastica>

The Coffee Source is a green bean coffee trading company that connects global companies with local coffee producers and growers. In 2018 they approached us to rebrand and redesign their company and thus launch a new website. My initial role was to help create and define some branding assets. Yet, my biggest contribution was to design and frame their new site from the wireframes, hi-fi prototype tool and to supervise the developing team.

This website's purpose was to inform and showcase what The Coffee Source is and what they stand for. Thus, there is a lot of small interactions to keep the users active and curious to learn more about the company. For instance having the world's map and showcase their client and producer's portfolio. There was a lot of thought put into the pacing and 'scrollability' of the site, having small pauses with the full bleed backgrounds with impactful statements.
